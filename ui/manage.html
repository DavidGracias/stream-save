Important Notes:
  • For single episodes, use format: imdbid:season:episode (e.g., tt0944947:1:8)
  • For entire series, just use the IMDB ID (e.g., tt0944947)
  • Stream links can be magnet links, direct URLs, or YouTube links
Examples:
Movie: tt0468569 (The Dark Knight)
Series: tt0944947 (Game of Thrones)
Episode: tt0944947:1:8 (GoT S1E8)
Supported formats:
• Magnet links: magnet:?xt=urn:btih:...
• Direct URLs: https://example.com/video.mp4
• YouTube: https://youtube.com/watch?v=...

Content Management Help
How to find IMDB IDs:
  1. Go to IMDB.com
  2. Search for your movie or series
  3. Copy the ID from the URL (starts with "tt")
  4. Example: https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0944947/ → tt0944947
For individual episodes:

Use the format: imdbid:season:episode

Example: tt0944947:1:8 for Game of Thrones Season 1 Episode 8

Supported stream formats:
  • Magnet links: magnet:?xt=urn:btih:...
  • Direct URLs: https://example.com/video.mp4
  • YouTube: https://youtube.com/watch?v=...
